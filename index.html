Our Dental Services

Complete care from prevention to restoration to cosmetic enhancement.

🔍
Dental Consultation
A thorough oral examination to assess your dental health and develop a personalized treatment plan.
  • Comprehensive oral health assessment
  • X-rays and diagnostic imaging
  • Customized treatment planning
  • Preventive care recommendations
🦷
Root Canal Treatment
Advanced painless root canal procedures using modern techniques to save your natural tooth.
  • Painless modern procedure
  • Single or multiple sitting options
  • Post-treatment care guidance
  • High success rate
🩺
Tooth Extraction
Safe, comfortable extraction with local anesthesia and expert aftercare instructions.
  • Simple and surgical extractions
  • Wisdom tooth removal
  • Minimal discomfort procedure
  • Post-extraction care support
✨
Teeth Cleaning (Scaling)
Professional scaling and polishing to remove plaque, tartar and stains for healthier gums.
  • Ultrasonic scaling technology
  • Removes tartar and plaque
  • Polishing for brighter teeth
  • Gum disease prevention
🔧
Dental Fillings
Tooth-colored composite fillings that blend naturally with your smile to restore decayed teeth.
  • Tooth-colored composite
  • Cavity restoration
  • Quick and painless
  • Long-lasting durability
👑
Dental Crowns
Custom-fitted crowns that protect damaged teeth while looking completely natural.
  • Porcelain and ceramic options
  • Natural color matching
  • Protects weakened teeth
  • Long-lasting solution
💎
Cosmetic Dentistry
Smile enhancement procedures to give you the confident, beautiful smile you deserve.
  • Teeth whitening
  • Smile makeover
  • Veneers and bonding
  • Gap closure treatment
🛡️
Preventive Dentistry
Proactive care to maintain optimal oral health and prevent costly dental problems.
  • Regular checkup scheduling
  • Fluoride treatment
  • Oral hygiene education
  • Early problem detection
